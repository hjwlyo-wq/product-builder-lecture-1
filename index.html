    Detailed Guide: Dog-Face vs. Cat-Face

    🐕 The Dog-Look Characteristics

    Dog-faced individuals often have a friendly, approachable, and trustworthy appearance. Key features analyzed by our AI include:

    • Rounded eye shapes that convey warmth.
    • A soft jawline and slightly rounded facial contour.
    • Bright, energetic expressions that suggest an extroverted personality.

    🐈 The Cat-Look Characteristics

    Cat-faced individuals are characterized by their chic, mysterious, and sharp features. Our AI looks for:

    • Sharp, slightly tilted outer corners of the eyes.
    • A defined and clear facial structure, often with prominent cheekbones.
    • Cool, calm, and sophisticated expressions that suggest a more reserved or observant nature.
